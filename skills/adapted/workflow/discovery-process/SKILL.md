# Discovery Process Workflow

**Purpose:** End-to-end customer discovery that leads to validated problems and opportunities.

**Key Concepts:** Continuous discovery, interview techniques, synthesis, opportunity mapping.

**Application:** Use at the start of any new initiative or when feeding agentic research agents.

**Examples:** Mapping clinician workflows in large healthcare networks to identify high-friction areas like inbox triage.

**Common Pitfalls:** Stopping too early, leading with solutions, ignoring regulated constraints.

**References:** Teresa Torres, Marty Cagan.

**Product Edge Extension:** Full EDGE Empathize → Define cycle.  
**Agentic Upgrade:** The vibe-to-ship orchestrator runs this workflow automatically with researcher + critic agents.  
**Regulated Example:** Used extensively in clinical workflow and cybersecurity discovery projects.
