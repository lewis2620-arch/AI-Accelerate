# Agentic Discharge & Care Transition Coordinator – Large Healthcare Network

**Category:** Multi-System Care Orchestration  
**North Star:** Zero-defect transitions from hospital to home.  

**Problem:** Manual medication reconciliation, scheduling, and follow-up gaps.  

**Solution:** Automated reconciliation validation, follow-up scheduling, readmission risk alerts, patient education routing, and home-health coordination with clinician approval.  

**Impact:** Lower readmissions, better quality metrics.  

**Product Edge Alignment:** EDGE full cycle + PLG expansion loops.  
**Agentic Extension:** Multi-agent mesh with external API tools.  
**Key Learnings:** Real outcomes come from end-to-end workflow orchestration, not isolated features.
