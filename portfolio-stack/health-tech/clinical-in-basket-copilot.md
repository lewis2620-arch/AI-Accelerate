# Clinical In-Basket AI Copilot – Large Healthcare Network

**Category:** Burnout Reduction Agent  
**North Star:** Turn inbox overload into prioritized, safe action for clinicians.  

**Problem:** High-volume messages across EHR, orders, and alerts causing administrative burden.  

**Solution:** Context-aware triage, risk prioritization, draft responses, order suggestions, and medication safety checks with human-in-the-loop approval.  

**Impact:** Reduced admin time, higher clinician productivity and satisfaction.  

**Product Edge Alignment:** PRISM (responsible AI + HITL) + EDGE Govern phase.  
**Agentic Extension:** Uses memory of patient history and policy rules.  
**Key Learnings:** Agentic systems shine when they augment judgment, not replace it.
