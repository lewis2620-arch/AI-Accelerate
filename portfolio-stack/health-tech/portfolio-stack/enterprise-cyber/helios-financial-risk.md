# Helios – Agentic Financial Risk Platform – Enterprise Finance Team

**Category:** Financial Compliance & Risk Automation
**North Star:** Continuous monitoring and automated regulatory reporting.

## Problem
Manual portfolio monitoring and compliance checks at scale. Risk analysts spending weeks generating reports that are outdated by the time they're distributed.

## Solution
Continuous financial risk monitoring system:
- Anomaly detection agent (portfolio drift, threshold breaches)
- Risk scoring and classification agent
- Automated regulatory report generation
- Audit trail for all monitoring decisions
- Escalation routing to risk officers for Tier 1 findings

## Impact
- Faster risk response — alerts in minutes vs. weekly reports
- Reduced compliance overhead for reporting cycles
- Audit-ready documentation generated automatically

## Framework Alignment
- **PRISM**: Governance layer as core infrastructure — OCC SR 11-7 alignment
- **FITR**: Wedge entry through reporting automation, expanded to monitoring
- **5-Layer Stack**: Layers 3 (governance), 5 (deployment controls)

## Key Learnings
In financial services, the audit trail IS the product. Regulators don't care how smart the risk model is — they care whether every decision is documented, explainable, and reversible.
