# Agentic Population Health Navigator – Large Healthcare Network

**Category:** Risk Stratification + Value-Based Care
**North Star:** Proactive chronic care management and shared-savings optimization.

## Problem
Reactive outreach model — care gaps identified after they become expensive. Manual care-gap identification taking weeks, by which time intervention windows have closed.

## Solution
Predictive population health agent system:
- Admission risk modeling agent (chronic disease + social determinants)
- Automated care pathway orchestration
- Coordinated outreach scheduling with care team routing
- Shared-savings attribution and reporting
- Clinician-in-loop for all high-risk patient interventions

## Impact
- Improved chronic disease outcomes in value-based care populations
- Financial alignment improvement in shared-savings contracts
- Care team time redirected from reactive to proactive interventions

## Framework Alignment
- **EDGE**: Full cycle with population health analyst and clinical stakeholder discovery
- **PLG**: Network effect — more patient data improves risk model, improves outreach, improves outcomes
- **PRISM**: Bias audit required — risk models must not disadvantage protected populations
